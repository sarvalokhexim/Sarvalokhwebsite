PROCESSING CAPABILITIES & FORMATS

Anatomical Specification & Formats

We comply with precise anatomical requirements for global markets. Below are our five primary export configurations.

Processing Type Trade Code Anatomical Description Standard Packing
Head-On Shell-On HOSO 100% natural intact state. Includes head, shell armor, antenna, and full tail fan. Block / Semi-IQF
Headless Shell-On HLSO Head is removed cleanly. The body shell segments and tail fan remain completely intact. Block Frozen
Easy-Peel Formats EZ-PEEL Headless shrimp with a uniform mechanical line cut down the back shell for quick peeling. IQF / Polybags
Peeled Deveined Tail-On PDTO Head and body shell completely extracted. Back vein removed. Final tail segment left on. IQF / Retail Ready
Peeled & Deveined P&D 100% naked shrimp meat. Head, shell, and tail fans are completely removed and deveined. IQF / Bulk Packs

SHIPPING, COLD CHAIN & LOGISTICS

Global Logistics Standards

Maintain -25°C

Freezing Formats

Available in IQF (Individually Quick Frozen) for retail portioning or Block Frozen (1.8kg / 2.0kg blocks) for optimum ocean voyage glaze protection.

Master Packaging Configurations

Standard US configuration: 10 bags x 2 lbs = 20 lbs Net Master Carton.
Standard EU configuration: 10 boxes x 1 kg = 10 kg Net Master Carton.

Ocean Logistics & Containers

Shipped exclusively via 40-foot High-Cube Reefer Containers maintained continuously at a strict cold chain baseline of -20°C to -25°C.

Net Weight Guarantee & Glazing

Glazing customizable from 5% to 30% depending on transit distance. Full compliance invoicing ensures a 100% Net Weight turnout upon container degrazing.
